New Zealand

Study in New Zealand

Slowly but steadily, New Zealand has conquered its position in the top study destinations in the world. The Government of New Zealand highly emphasizes on the education quality of New Zealand and therefore it is drawing attention of international students.  Considering the number of educational bodies then 8-10 universities of New Zealand are included in the top 500 universities of the world. With the safest and beautiful living environment, New Zealand has now become one of the leading study destinations and fascinated hefty number of students from all over the world. However, acquiring New Zealand Study Visa involves many formalities ranging from acceptance letter to final Visa Process.

Along with the world-class education system of New Zealand, it also offers incomparable opportunities to students in regards to placements and further job prospects. The quality of education in New Zealand is improving by every year and this is considered as the crucial factor of rising number of international students in the country.

 

Why Study in New Zealand?

There are numerous reasons to choose New Zealand as your study abroad destination as it is combination of quality as well as leisure. Being a student in New Zealand, one can experience a wonderful life leading to much better lifestyle.

 

World-Ranked Universities

Based on the pragmatic approach and innovative teaching methods, Education system of New Zealand is world-wide recognized. The universities and colleges of New Zealand are government approved and provide high quality education to the national as well as international students.

 

Flexible Entry Requirements

New Zealand offers you flexible living environment as the universities and colleges provide equivalent opportunities to the students. Thus, international students can easily apply in top-ranked universities of New Zealand. Although, these requirements depends upon the university and program you choose.

 

Easy Visa Process

In order to avoid any challenges and hassles, New Zealand has prepared a much rationalized process that assures smooth and easy visa processing. Many New Zealand universities and colleges help students in meeting post arrival formalities and even help in visa renewal and extension services as well.

 

Cost-Effective Option

The Education System of New Zealand is entirely based on British Education and you can attain it at very affordable cost.  The education in New Zealand is comparatively cheaper than US, UK and other countries.

 

Welcoming Living Environment

New Zealand has extremely welcoming environment for international students as there is multicultural living environment. Students from different parts of the world are studying and living in New Zealand.

Top Universities & Colleges

Universities and colleges in New Zealand are based on the latest education norms and thus renowned for offering excellent education quality. Moreover, there are eight top ranked universities in New Zealand which comes under QS World University Rakings.

Tuition Fees & Living Cost

The tuition fees and living cost in New Zealand is comparatively lower than countries like Australia and US but offers equivalent quality of education and lifestyle. The tuition fees for bachelor degree in New Zealand start from NZ $ 22,000 and a master degree from NZ $ 26,000. 

Average Living expenses in New Zealand is NZ$1250 per month. This cost is majorly based upon your lifestyle and personal expenses. 

 

Average Income

The average wage rate is quite good as compared to other countries which are $14.75 per hour. This will help students to bear their living expenses in New Zealand.
